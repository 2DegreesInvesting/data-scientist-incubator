Introduction

This book contains materials presented at ds-incubator meetups.

The goal of the ds-incubator (data science incubator) is to share best practices in coding. It helps us to become better data scientists and to improve out work at at 2° Investing Initiative.

This project is managed by Mauro with support from CJ and guidance from Klaus and Clare. Since mid-October, 2019 we meetup online weekly, and we plan a personal meetup in 2020. We welcome contributions from everyone at 2° Investing Initiative.
